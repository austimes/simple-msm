# Supply domestic solid fuels Validation

The residual incumbent method covers the domestic carrier supply boundary with a one-unit residual activity anchor in every milestone year. It validates package completeness for the physical role graph without changing the 2025 fuel or emissions closure.

Baseline direct emissions are intentionally not reallocated to this role. Existing residual mining-energy and fugitive-emissions roles keep upstream releases explicit, avoiding double counting until richer supply pathways are available.
