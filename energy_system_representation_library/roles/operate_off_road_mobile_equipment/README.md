# Operate off-road mobile equipment

## What the role represents

This role keeps remaining non-road transport visible after rail, aviation, and marine freight have their own minimum coverage roles. It is currently represented by one residual incumbent method.

## Role definition

- Role id: `operate_off_road_mobile_equipment`
- Balance type: `service_demand`
- Output unit: `residual_activity`
- Output quantity basis: One residual activity unit carrying the other non-road share of the former transport-other calibration.
- Default incumbent method id: `transport_other_non_road__residual_incumbent`

## Expansion path

Replace the residual method with explicit off-road, pipeline, service, and other transport pathways when activity and energy evidence is authored.
