# Account soil fertiliser and residue emissions

Residual role split from product-output or aggregate residual agriculture and land-sector calibration rows. The role has one route, `Residual incumbent`, and a 2025 demand anchor of `1 residual_activity`.

The purpose is to keep soil fertiliser and residue emissions visible as explicit physical coverage rather than embedding it in product-output roles or aggregate residual buckets.

Growth proxy is low-confidence and should be replaced when explicit biological or land-stock drivers are authored.
