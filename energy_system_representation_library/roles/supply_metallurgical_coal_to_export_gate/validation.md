# Supply metallurgical coal to export gate Validation

The residual incumbent method covers the Australian export-gate coal supply boundary with a one-unit residual activity anchor in every milestone year. It validates package completeness for the physical role graph without adding overseas combustion emissions.

Coal mine fugitive methane is intentionally not reallocated here. The existing `account_residual_fugitives` role keeps those releases visible, avoiding double counting until richer export coal pathways are available.
